Privacy Policy

The Smart Water Point Monitoring System collects the account and location information you provide during registration (name, contact details, and administrative area) solely to operate this water point monitoring platform for Bugesera District.

Your data is used to authenticate your account, attribute reports and maintenance actions, and route notifications relevant to your role and district. It is not shared with third parties and is retained only for as long as your account remains active.

By registering, you consent to this use of your information within the system.
